### What is InfraWatch?

InfraWatch is a self-hosted monitoring platform for AWS with **zero standing access** — built for DevOps engineers, SREs, and platform teams who care about security and observability.

- **Real-time monitoring** across 12+ AWS services (EC2, EKS, RDS, ElastiCache, and more)
- **JIT access control** with request-approval workflows and auto-expiring STS credentials
- **Plugin architecture** with 7 built-in plugins and an open SDK
- **OpenTelemetry-native** monitor agent for logs, metrics, and traces

No vendor lock-in. No per-seat pricing. Your infrastructure, your data.
